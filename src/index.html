Instructions Lite Account
  1. Register a lite account in the Felix Lite Registration screen selecting 1 or more subscriptions.
  2. Register the same lite account in the Nescafé Lite Registration screen selecting 1 or more subscriptions.
  3. Authenticate with the lite account in the Preferences Center Login screen.
  4. Check your email's inbox and click on the link inside to be redirected to the Profile Update screen
Instructions Full Account
  1. Register an account in the Felix Registration screen selecting 1 or more subscriptions.
  2. Login with the same account in the Nescafé Login screen, then select 1 or more subscriptions in the Registration Completion screen.
  3. Logout before trying to login on the preferences center
  4. Login in the Preferences Center Login screen and accept the terms of service in the Registration Completion screen.
  5. Access the subscriptions in the Profile Update screen.
